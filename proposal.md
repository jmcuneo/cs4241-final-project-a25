We plan to make an interactive card game website in which players can play the game War against eachother. The server will maintain a gamestate, which holds the cards each play has, and will update as each player plays cards. It will also maintain score, turn, etc.
We are unsure about specific authentication processes, but we plan to have a database to keep track of wins and losses between players. The frontend will display the number of cards you have in your pile to play, as will as the cards you have won so far, and a score between player one and player two. Each turn will have a count down in the frontend, and each player needs to click on the top of their deck during that countdown to play their card. Following the rules of War, the player who plays the higher card will take the cards into their point pile. In the case of a war, each player will pull 3 cards from their deck and choose which of the three cards to flip, and the person with the higher of the two flipped cards will win all cards in contestion.
